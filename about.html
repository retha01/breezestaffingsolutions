Through unwavering dedication, hands-on experience, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, he has transformed from student to industry leader, exemplifying the power of commitment and continuous growth in achieving professional success.

Today, Thula has cultivated exceptional expertise in staff management, team coordination, and operational leadership within the events and marketing landscape.
